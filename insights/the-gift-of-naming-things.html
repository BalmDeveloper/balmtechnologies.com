The Gift of Naming Things

April 1, 2026

There is a simple but powerful gift in naming things: when you give an idea a name, it becomes real and easier to understand, share, and grow.

As humans, it is our job to notice the ideas that come to us and give them words, because naming is the first step in bringing something to life.

A name gives direction and meaning—it shows that an idea matters. Many of the things we see in the world today started as small, unnamed thoughts until someone chose to name them.

So when an idea comes to you, even if it feels unclear, will you ignore it, or will you name it and give it a chance to grow?
